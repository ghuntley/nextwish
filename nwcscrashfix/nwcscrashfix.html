One of those cheats is actually a Denial of Service attack, which when a cheater uses it on the server that you are playing on it either makes your half-life crash, requiring you to restart your computer or it kicks you from the server complaining about hostage skins being incorrect. Unfortunatly, 2 Versions of Counter-Strike have past and the exploit has yet to be fixed, so this is where NextWish's Counter-Strike Crash fix comes into play.
