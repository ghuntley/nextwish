About this modification:
There's no doubt you have heard of Tribes 2, the sequel to the best online multiplayer game ever created. Anyways this modification replaces most of the sounds in Tribes 1 with the ones from Tribes 2!

Installation Instructions
1. Simply point the installer to your main Tribes 1 directory. 

2. Load up Tribes 1 and Enjoy!
